About us

Established in 1980 by industry stalwart R.V. Manikandan, Sri Ganesh Papers stands as a testament to decades of experience and unwavering dedication to the paper manufacturing industry. With over 40 years of expertise in the trade, R.V. Manikandan brings unparalleled knowledge and insight to the helm of the company.

At Sri Ganesh Papers, we take pride in our diverse product portfolio, offering a comprehensive range of paper solutions to meet the varied needs of our clients. From MF papers to specialized products such as thermal paper, MICR paper, and tissue paper, we strive to provide high-quality materials that exceed industry standards.

Innovation lies at the core of our operations. We continuously invest in cutting-edge technology and processes to stay ahead of the curve and deliver innovative paper solutions to our customers. Our commitment to innovation drives us to explore new avenues and push boundaries in the paper manufacturing landscape.
